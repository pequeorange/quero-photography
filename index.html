Quero Photography art, images, memories

Michigan-based photographer capturing the essence of the moments of life and storing them in your mind through pictures

Ramiro Quero

I like to create artistic impressions of your experience for you to reconnect with every time you see your pictures. I have always liked photography, but it wasn't until my wife gifted me a camera for my birthday that I delved deeply into creating photographs that attract the eye and the mind. My pictures aren't 100% perfect from the technical side of things, but life isn't either. To borrow a mentor's term, my photographs are "perfectly incomeplete." I believe your life is too, but that's the beauty of it all.

Family

Being a family man is hands down my favorite role. I married the love of my life, Tara, and we have now two new loves--Beni & Juni. Life gets richer and richer as you grow.

Teacher

I taught high school for almost 9 yers. My friends sometimes remind me I don't have to teach them anything. I love sharing what I learn, especially photography.

Photography

I was 29 (or 28?) when my wife gifted me a Nikon D750. My photographer friends gave me lots of tutorials for free (thanks a lot!), and I started practicing. I crave feedback on my pictures, so I ask if from anyone who can help me grow. I continue to grow as a photographer, so I thank you in advance for helping me grow.

Photography Services

I enjoy learning new styles and tehcniques, if you have an idea of a service not outlined below, I'd love to help you make it a reality.

  • Senior Solo Shoot

    Congratulations! You have made it... almost! Celebrate by doing a fun session. Remember, this is really for your family to brag about you.

  • Senior Group Shoot

    Same as above for for the whole gang (remember, bulk is always cheaper).

  • Couple Session

    Bring your loved one on a delightful shoot to celebrate, to update your album, to post online, or to put in your Christmas cards.

  • Professional Portraits

    Do you need a professional pictures to update your resume? Studio quality pictures may help you stand out amongst the crowd.

  • Family Session

    Dad, mom, kids, fur kids, all or some of the above? Sure thing! Let's memorialize it as it is now. Life keeps flying by, save the memory of the now.

  • Weddings/Elopements

    Arguably the best day of your life. As the years by, the details will become blurry. Prevent that by capturing the quintessence of it all.

This is your shoot, and I help you make it a reality. Please let me know of any questions I haven't answered. I would honor to work for you in creating art, images, and memories for you and yours.
